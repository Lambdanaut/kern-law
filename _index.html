At Richard Kern Law our attorneys work closely with clients dealing with legal problems in North Carolina. We take great care and pride in maintaining a client focused practice.

In addition to handling the issues of separation, child support, child custody, post separation support, alimony, equitable distribution, breach of separation agreements, domestic violence, and divorce matters for you, we also can help with your minor traffic and criminal infractions or land use and zoning issues.

We want to be your family attorney, there for you when you need help and guidance through all the legal pitfalls and strife that life can throw at you. We make it a point to not only handle your matter thoroughly and efficiently, but to take the time to explain every aspect of the law with respect to your issue so that your stress and anxiety is reduced.
